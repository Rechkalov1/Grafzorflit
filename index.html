Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start a new game?

A: Simply navigate to the 'Games' section, choose your desired game, and click 'Play Now'. Most games will start instantly.

Q: Are there any in-game purchases?

A: Grafzorflit is a completely free-to-play social gaming platform. There are no real money purchases or transactions within any of our games.

Q: How can I add friends?

A: You can add friends by searching for their username within the 'Friends' section or by clicking on their profile in game leaderboards.

Q: What are clans and how do I join one?

A: Clans are groups of players who team up for quests and social interaction. You can join an existing clan or create your own from the 'Clans' menu.

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

A: On the login screen, click 'Forgot Password' and follow the instructions to reset it using your registered email address.

Q: Can I change my username?

A: Usernames cannot be changed after creation to maintain community consistency. Please choose carefully when signing up.
